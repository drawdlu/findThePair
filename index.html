FindThePair

Here's how to play:

  1. Choose a card. Click on any card to turn it face up.
  2. Find its match. Look for another card with the same picture.
  3. Try to remember. If you find a match, both cards will stay face up. If not, they'll be turned face down again.
  4. Keep going. Continue until you've matched all the pairs.

The goal is to find all the pairs as quickly as possible. It's a fun way to test your memory and concentration!
